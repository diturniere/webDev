Male sandwich

  • Time: 15 minutes
Ingredients Quantiy
Long loaf 1
Any ready-made meat ~by your taste
Hard cheese 70g
Tomato 1
Any sauce ~by your taste

Process: We cut the loaf into two flat parts. In the lower half, we make a depression, removing the crumb. Lubricate the depression with sauce. Place 4-5 slices of meat in the middle. Cut the tomatoes into slices, put them on the sausage. Put the meat again. Lubricate the meat with sauce. Put a couple of slices of cheese on the meat. You can additionally grease the edges with sauce. Cover the half filled with vegetables and meat with the other half. We put our super sandwich in the microwave or oven. We bake until tender, until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.


Chicken curry

  • Time: 60 minutes
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken fillet 2
Onion 1
Ground red pepper ~by your taste
Lime 1
Curry 2 teaspoons

Process: Cut the fillet into small pieces. Finely chop the onion, add a little hot pepper. Fry until golden brown in olive oil. Add the chicken, fry over high heat for 3-5 minutes. Sprinkle with curry, squeeze out the juice of a medium-sized lime (large - half) and simmer over medium heat for about 40 minutes.
